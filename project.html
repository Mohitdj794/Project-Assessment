Business Class

Business class is a class of seating on an airplane that is more expensive than economy but less expensive than first class. Generally, business class is indented for corporate travelers. The difference between first class and economy usually lies in service, seats and food.

Economy class

Economy class is another term for the airplane's main cabin, as opposed to premium cabins like business class and first class. Sometimes referred to as coach class, economy class typically makes up the bulk of the seating on a flight. It's the most simple class, with the fewest amenities.
